What is Digital Literacy?

Digital Literacy is regarded as the ability to find, evaluate, utilize, share, and create content using information technologies and the Internet (Cornell University). In a similar fashion, digital education is the innovative use of digital tools and technologies during teaching and learning. It is otherwise referred to as Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL) or e-Learning. Digital literacy affords educators the opportunity to design relevant, engaging learning experiences in their various subject areas and deliver lessons in a more efficient way. Digital education can either be taken fully online or together with the traditional form as a blended classroom. It is expected that 21st century learners would take a cue from its import and adjust their learning in the right direction.

Today, more and more schools and teachers have started to recognize and appreciate the significance of the internet in assisting them to prepared and deliver great content both online and offline.In the current situation of information technology, especially following COVID-19,online teaching has spanned all levels and kinds of education in a bid to mitigate the restrictions placed on movement and social interactions following the outbreak of the pandemic in late 2019.
